{% trans "About SimpleValidations" %}

SimpleValidations is the developed by McQuillen Interactive, a Melbourne-based web application studio founded by Daniel McQuillen.

I have spent the past two decades building practical software for research labs, building scientists, and educators who care about craft as much as they care about data accuracy.

The idea for SimpleValidations first took shape while creating a glazing system validation tool for the Windows and Daylighting Group at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. Watching that tool save engineers hours of back-and-forth convinced me that smarter, kinder validation workflows could help any team that wrestles with complex data submissions.

{% trans "What I'm Building" %}

My goal is to give product teams, analysts, and domain experts a dependable co-pilot that makes data quality checks feel routine instead of punitive. SimpleValidations blends automated rules, AI-assisted review, and clear audit trails so you can trust what you ship without adding another layer of process overhead. SimpleValidations hides complex logic or simulations behind a simple interface so you and your users can validate data and documents with confidence and ease.

{% trans "Why It Matters" %}

Whether I am helping an energy-efficiency researcher, a nonprofit educator, or a commercial product team, the same themes surface: people want software that respects their time, reflects their domain language, and keeps stakeholders aligned. SimpleValidations carries that ethos forward - crafting an opinionated, humane workflow so teams can trust their data, respond faster, and focus on the work only humans can do.

{% trans "What's Next" %}

I'm working with a select group of early users to make sure the system provides value and fits naturally into their workflows. If this all sounds interesting, I would love to hear how SimpleValidations can support you.
