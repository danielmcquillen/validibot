Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 April 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how McQuillen Interactive Pty Ltd (ABN #### ) ("SimpleValidations," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal information in connection with the SimpleValidations software-as-a-service platform, websites, mobile applications, APIs, and related support services (collectively, the "Services"). We are an Australian company headquartered in Victoria, and we provide Services to customers in Australia, the United States, and other jurisdictions.

1. Scope and Roles

SimpleValidations acts as the "APP entity" under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and as the "data controller" for personal information we collect directly from prospects, users, and visitors. Where our customers submit personal information to the Services, SimpleValidations acts as a "data processor" (or "service provider") on the customer's behalf. Customers remain responsible for obtaining the necessary rights to submit personal information to the Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account information. Name, employer, role, email address, authentication credentials, billing contacts, and similar information provided when you create or manage an account.
  • Payment information. Limited payment details such as billing address and transaction identifiers processed via our PCI-compliant payment partners; we do not store full credit card numbers.
  • Customer Data. Files, datasets, and metadata uploaded or generated through the Services, including audit logs, workflow configurations, validation history, and related comments.
  • Usage and device data. Log data, IP address, browser type, device identifiers, time zone, referral URLs, pages viewed, and interactions collected through cookies and analytics tools when you access the Services.
  • Support and communications. Information you submit via email, chat, or support tickets, including recordings of support calls where permitted.
  • Marketing information. Preferences, event registrations, feedback, and marketing communications history.

3. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide, configure, and maintain the Services;
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts;
  • Process transactions, invoicing, and subscription renewals;
  • Deliver customer support, training, and professional services;
  • Monitor system performance, troubleshoot issues, and develop new features;
  • Send operational notices, security alerts, and administrative messages;
  • Conduct product research, analytics, and aggregated reporting;
  • Market and promote the Services consistent with your communication preferences; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

For individuals located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or similar jurisdictions, we rely on the following legal bases: (a) performance of a contract when we provide the Services; (b) compliance with legal obligations; (c) legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving our business; and (d) consent for optional uses such as certain marketing communications or cookies where required.

5. How We Share Information

We share personal information only with:

  • Service providers. Trusted vendors supplying hosting, payment processing, analytics, customer support, email delivery, and other operational services bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Integration partners. Third-party applications you choose to connect to the Services. Data exchanged with integration partners is subject to their privacy policies.
  • Professional advisors. Lawyers, accountants, auditors, and insurers assisting us in managing our business.
  • Corporate transactions. An acquiring or merging entity, with appropriate safeguards, if we undergo a business transition.
  • Legal and regulatory authorities. When required by applicable law, lawful request, or to protect rights, property, safety, or enforce agreements.

6. International Data Transfers

We host data primarily in Australia and the United States. If we transfer personal information across borders, including to countries that may not provide equivalent data protection, we will implement appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses, intra-group agreements, or reliance on adequacy decisions. By using the Services, you authorise SimpleValidations to process and transfer personal information globally in accordance with this Policy.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to deliver the Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Customer Data is retained in accordance with the applicable subscription plan and is deleted or anonymised within 30 days after termination unless we are legally required or permitted to retain it longer.

8. Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Measures include encryption in transit, role-based access controls, logging and monitoring, secure software development practices, regular vulnerability assessments, and incident response procedures. No system is fully secure; we encourage you to use strong passwords and enable multi-factor authentication where available.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, update, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal information, and to obtain a copy of your data in a portable format. Australian users may request access or correction under the Privacy Act 1988. Individuals in the EEA, UK, and similar regions have rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. California residents may exercise rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@simplevalidations.com. We will verify requests and respond within applicable timelines.

10. Marketing Communications

We may send you marketing emails about new features, events, or promotions. You can opt out at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the message or by contacting us. Even if you opt out of marketing communications, we may still send essential transactional or security notices.

11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to authenticate users, remember preferences, analyse usage, and improve the Services. You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies, though doing so may impact certain features. Where required, we will obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

12. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to or intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information may contact us for assistance.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our Services. When we make material changes, we will provide notice through the Services or by email. Your continued use of the Services after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

14. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, contact us at privacy@simplevalidations.com or by mail at McQuillen Interactive Pty Ltd, Unit 7, 3 Bolinda Street, Bentleight, Victoria 3204, Australia. If you are located in the EEA or UK, you also have the right to raise concerns with your local data protection authority.
